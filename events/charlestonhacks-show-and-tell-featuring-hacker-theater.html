How Nearify works at this event

Nearify is the live connection layer for in-person events. The app helps you discover attendees during the event, and the web experience will later help turn those interactions into follow-up, memory, and network intelligence.

1

Install and sign in

Get Nearify on TestFlight, sign in with Google or GitHub, and create your profile so people can find and understand you.

2

Join the event

Scan the event QR code at the venue to enter the live event network and reveal the attendees participating in this session.

3

Discover who is here

See other attendees, explore who feels relevant, and move from passive attendance to intentional discovery.

4

Find and connect

Tap a person, use proximity guidance, and connect when you are in range. The interaction becomes part of your event network.

Why use Nearify instead of just mingling?

Great events create a lot of possibility, but most of that value disappears when you do not know who is there, who matters to you, or how to reconnect later. Nearify helps make the room legible.

Without Nearify

  • You meet people by chance
  • You forget who was in the room
  • Good conversations are hard to follow up on
  • The event ends and the network disappears

With Nearify

  • You discover who is actually around you
  • You connect intentionally in real space
  • Your interactions become a persistent network
  • Future event intelligence can build from what happened
